Player App

1) Microphone which can record a high quality vocals. Could be interesting for people who want to add their voice over the original song.
2) Choose a playlist. You are able to create different playlists, so it would be easier to find your favorite songs.
3) Play a song.
4) Pause.
5) Radio. Listen any station online.
6) No sound.
7) Silent.
8) Low volume.
9) High volume.
10) Stop.
